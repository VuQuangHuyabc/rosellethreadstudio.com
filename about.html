Our Story

From a small workshop to a trusted name in quality threads

Our Beginning

Roselle Thread Studio LLC began as a small family-owned business with a simple mission: to provide crafters and textile artists with the finest quality threads and yarns available. Our founders, themselves passionate about the art of threadwork, recognized a need for a reliable source of premium materials.

Starting from our workshop in Santa Fe, New Mexico, we carefully curated each product in our collection, ensuring that every thread and yarn meets our exacting standards for quality, durability, and color vibrancy.

Our Growth

Over the years, we've grown from a local supplier to a trusted name serving customers across the United States and beyond. Our commitment to quality has never wavered, and we continue to expand our product range to meet the evolving needs of the crafting community.

Today, Roselle Thread Studio LLC stands as a testament to the power of passion, quality, and customer service. We're proud to be part of countless creative projects and to support the artistic endeavors of our valued customers.
