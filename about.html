About Roselle Thread Studio LLC

Roselle Thread Studio LLC is a fashion brand dedicated to creating elegant and timeless women's dresses that blend comfort with modern style.

Our company is officially registered in the United States under the name Roselle Thread Studio LLC, with a business address at 30 N Gould St Ste N, Sheridan, WY 82801. We specialize in designing and offering high-quality women's dresses, with a focus on long wool dresses and everyday elegant wear.

Our Story

From a small boutique to a trusted name in quality dresses

Our Philosophy

At Roselle Thread Studio LLC, we believe that a dress is more than just clothing. It is a way to express confidence, personality, and individual style. That is why we pay close attention to materials, details, and design to ensure every product meets our standards of quality and aesthetics.

Each piece is carefully crafted to provide a flattering fit, soft feel, and lasting comfort, making it suitable for both daily use and special occasions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to deliver products that are not only stylish but also practical and versatile for modern women around the world. We strive to create dresses that empower women to feel confident and beautiful in their everyday lives.

Through our commitment to quality craftsmanship and thoughtful design, we continue to expand our collection to meet the diverse needs and preferences of our valued customers.
