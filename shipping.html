Shipping Policy

Information about shipping options and delivery times

Last updated: December 2024

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days to ensure timely delivery for our customers.

Shipping Options Available

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, with expedited options available at checkout for faster delivery.

Transparent Shipping Policies

We provide clear shipping policies to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction with every order.

Shipping Rates

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $5.99 (orders under $50)
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days FREE (orders $50 and above)
Express Shipping 2-3 business days $12.99
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $24.99

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all addresses within the United States, including Alaska and Hawaii. International shipping is not available at this time.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on the carrier's website.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to meet the delivery times stated above, Roselle Thread Studio LLC cannot be held responsible for unexpected shipping delays caused by circumstances beyond our control, such as severe weather, natural disasters, or carrier delays.

Multiple Shipments

Occasionally, orders may be shipped in multiple packages if items are located in different warehouses. You will not be charged extra for this service.

Shipping Address

Please ensure that your shipping address is correct at the time of checkout. Roselle Thread Studio LLC is not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

Package Security

All packages are insured against loss or damage during transit. If your package arrives damaged or if you do not receive your package, please contact us immediately at contact@rosellethreadstudio.com.

PO Boxes and APO/FPO Addresses

We can ship to PO boxes and APO/FPO addresses via USPS. Please note that express shipping options may not be available for these addresses.

Important Notice

This Shipping Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Shipping Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to providing you with the best possible shipping experience. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team.
